this folder contains the data files for tropical cyclone and storm surge  
see https://github.com/davidnbresch and https://github.com/davidnbresch/climada_module_tropical_cyclone

Direct link to the climada manual (make yourself familiar with it first):
https://github.com/davidnbresch/climada/blob/master/docs/climada_manual.pdf

- ReefLocations.xlsx global location of reefs, used in surge (TS) moderation when reefs are in good shape (reef revival as an adaptation option)
- wind_decay_after_landfall_p_rel.mat parameters to modify cyclone intensity after landfall
- border_mask_10km.mat (old) global coastal border mask at 10k resolution
- coastline.txt (old) coastline (lat/lon) in text form
